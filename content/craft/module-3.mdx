<Section label="05 — B2B STAKEHOLDER CONFLICTS">
  <h3 className="sectionTitle">B2B Research & Buying Committees</h3>
  <div className="sectionSub">

  </div>

  <MindmapGrid>
    <MindmapNode defaultOpen title="The Economic Buyer" items='["Concerned with ROI, compliance, and budget consolidation", "Key question: Will this reduce total software spend or labor cost?", "Requires clean dashboarding and security reporting"]' />

<Section label="06 — ENGAGEMENT PSYCHOLOGY">
  <h3 className="sectionTitle">The Hook Model & Habit Formation</h3>
  <div className="sectionSub">
    Defensible products build habits. The Hook Model maps how external triggers transition into internal habits through investments.
  </div>

  <FlowWrapper>
    <FlowStep steps='[
      {"title": "1. Trigger", "sub": "External notification or internal emotional need (e.g. anxiety)"},
      {"title": "2. Action", "sub": "The simplest behavior done in anticipation of a reward"},
      {"title": "3. Variable Reward", "sub": "Immediate cognitive or social validation that satisfies the craving"},
      {"title": "4. Investment", "sub": "User puts data, work, or social capital back into the product"}
    ]' />
  </FlowWrapper>

  <Insight type="tradeoff" text="Variable rewards build habits, but they must align with genuine user utility. Manipulative feedback loops create short-term spikes followed by long-term churn." />

  <h3 className="sectionTitle">Problem-Solution Fit</h3>

  </div>

  <CompareGrid 
    left='{"title": "Unvalidated Assumptions", "items": ["Users will manually upload their CSV every Monday", "Managers will log in weekly to check analytics dashboards", "Developers will read docs before integrating APIs"]}'
